Hard News

The hottest stories lie behind the headlines.

Reporter Christie Bradley, beautiful and brave, is willing to take any risks for her job. It'll take a strong man to hold her.

David Cameron has been INN's top war correspondant for ten years. He's survived bullets and bombs, but he's never met the right woman.

Together they discover that passion is the hottest story of all.

But love is dangerous in a world where sex is the ultimate weapon.

Where the anchor position is more than just a job for newscaster Paragon Fairfax; where ruthless editor Ben Wordsworth would sell his soul for ratings.

And where Sir Edward Penhaligon, seemingly INN's one voice of integrity, plans his own, shocking revenge.
