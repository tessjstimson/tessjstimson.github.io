The Wife-in-Law

Coming Soon!

When Naomi Hartwell ran off with Penny’s husband, Joe, Penny swore she’d never forgive her. For six years, she’s been true to her word.

But then one morning Penny’s teenage daughter, Ellen, disappears with Naomi’s five-year-old son. The only clue is a frantic phone call from Ellen: ‘Don’t trust Dad.’

For the next thirty-six hours, the two women are forced to put their differences aside as they desperately search for their children. But the tenuous trust between them is soon under threat as they unravel a dangerous trail of family secrets.

And their fragile bond is tested to the limit by a shocking act of violence that threatens to destroy both their families.


Wife-in-law(waɪf.ɪn.lɔː)

n. 1 the wife of one’s ex-husband.

2 the ex-wife of one’s husband.

3 the only woman with as much to lose as you.
