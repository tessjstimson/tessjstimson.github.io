Welcome

I'm so pleased to welcome you to my website. Thanks for taking the time to check me out!

What extraordinary times we're living in. I know it's hard to concentrate on much of anything right now, but we all need a little escapism now more than ever. And a little murder never hurt anyone, right?

My new book, ONE IN THREE, is out on July 9, 2020 in ebook and audio (UK paperback will be available August 6.) One in three marriages end in divorce, but this one ends in murder!

The US paperback isn't available until the New Year, but in the meantime, A MOTHER'S SECRET might be just the appetiser you need. It's the story of every mother's worst fear...but it's not the story you know.

If you'd like to get in touch and tell me what you love or hate about my books, I'd love to hear from you. Just go to my contact page and drop me a line.

Please stay safe, and remember, no-one ever got sick curled up reading a book!

Tess, September 2020
