Journalism

I've worked as a news and features journalist for more than twenty years, writing as Tess Stimson for a host of newspapers and magazines including Woman, the Daily Mail, Mail on Sunday, Daily Express, The Times, You and Glamour. (Check out the antique Tandy to the right!)

Sex, adultery, the pain of losing your parents, the joy of buying shoes...I've covered them all.
